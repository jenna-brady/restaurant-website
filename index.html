About us

We treat you like family

Welcome to Bits ‘n Bytes, the place to go when you’re in need of a quick bite to eat. Located in T building at Algonquin college. We understand students are extremely busy and don’t have a lot of time to eat. Weather your looking for a quick snack or a wholesome meal. At Bits ‘n Bytes, we are very passionate about providing delicious food for our on the go students.
